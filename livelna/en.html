Adsumfluo Early access

Stay close to your students — even between classes.

Adsumfluo is a personal assistant that turns your everyday moments — a voice note after class, a photo between sessions — into polished posts, ready to publish. No content calendar. No editing skills. Just you, sharing what matters.

How it works

🎙

Send a voice note or a photo

You're leaving class and something comes to mind. Or you notice something your students would love. Open your favorite chat app, send a voice message, snap a photo. That's it — your part is done.

✏️

Your assistant shapes it into a post

The text gets written in your own words. The photo gets adjusted. A posting time is suggested. You get back a ready-to-publish post — not a rough draft, not a template.

🌱

Review, tweak, and let it go

Read it, change a word if you want, or just say 'looks good.' It goes to Instagram, to your own simple website, wherever your students look for you. You go back to what actually matters.

"I know exactly what I want to say — it just never makes it out of my head and onto the internet."

— yoga teacher, Budapest

No new app. No learning curve.

You already know how to send a voice message. That's all this takes. Your assistant lives in a chat app you already use — no login to remember, no interface to figure out. Just talk to it like you'd talk to a friend who happens to be great at social media.
