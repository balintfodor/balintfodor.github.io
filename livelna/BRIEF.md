# Adsumfluo — Project Brief

## What is this
A landing page for a early-stage SaaS product called Adsumfluo. The goal of this page is to validate interest — not to sell, not to explain every feature, but to make the right person feel understood and leave their email.

## What is Adsumfluo
A tool for independent yoga, fitness, tai-chi and bodywork instructors across the EU who struggle to maintain an online presence. It turns a post-class voice note or rough thought into a ready-to-publish post — in the instructor's own words — and sends it to Instagram, Telegram, and a simple personal website. One tap. Done.

## Who is this page for
A yoga or fitness instructor, typically 30s–40s, based in Hungary, Germany, Czech Republic or Slovakia. She teaches independently. She knows what she wants to say to her students but never gets it out of her head and onto the internet consistently. She is skeptical of generic AI tools. She values authenticity, simplicity, and privacy.

## What this page must do
1. Make her feel immediately understood
2. Explain what the product does in plain, concrete terms
3. Collect her email address
4. Ask one optional question: what is her biggest struggle with staying visible online

## What this page must NOT do
- Show a product mockup or screenshot (product does not exist yet)
- Mention AI prominently
- Feel like a tech startup landing page
- Use corporate "we" language
- Promise free access
- Be longer than one scrolled screen on mobile

## Success metric
A yoga teacher reads it and thinks: "this is exactly my problem." Then leaves her email.
